Who We Are

Anamta Furniture Crafters

Founded in the historic city of Jodhpur, Rajasthan — India's cradle of artisanal craftsmanship — Anamta Furniture Crafters is a premium furniture manufacturer dedicated to keeping the ancient art of wooden furniture-making alive.

We specialize in Sheesham wood furniture, luxury sofas, antique replica pieces, and completely custom furniture designed to your specifications. Every piece that leaves our workshop carries the fingerprints of skilled craftsmen who pour their hearts into every joint, carving, and finish.

Our showroom at G-617, IInd Phase Basni M.I.A, Jodhpur is open Monday through Sunday, welcoming you to touch, feel, and fall in love with furniture made the old way — with patience, skill, and genuine care.

The Material

Why Sheesham Wood?

Sheesham (Dalbergia sissoo), also known as Indian Rosewood, is one of the most prized hardwoods in the world. Its rich grain patterns, natural oils, and exceptional durability make it the ideal choice for heirloom-quality furniture.

  • ✦
    Naturally Termite Resistant
    The natural oils in Sheesham repel insects and resist rot.
  • ✦
    Exceptionally Hard & Durable
    Furniture that can last 50+ years with proper care.
  • ✦
    Beautiful Natural Grain
    Every piece has a unique grain pattern, making each one-of-a-kind.
  • ✦
    Eco-Friendly Choice
    Ethically sourced from sustainable plantations across Rajasthan.
